Hi, I’m Hendy — a photographer and videographer based in Bogor. Since 2019, I have been working in the visual industry to help clients capture their most valuable moments through photos and videos. I specialize in documenting gigs, product shoots, and graduation events.

My journey in photography and videography started with a simple desire: to capture the best moments during my mountain hiking adventures. Over time, this hobby grew into a passion and a profession. I believe that every moment holds a story and emotion worth preserving, and my mission is to capture it honestly and meaningfully.

My work focuses on a natural and storytelling approach, so that every photo and video not only looks good but also brings back the atmosphere and feelings of the moment. With experience since 2019, I am committed to delivering detailed, high-quality results that meet your expectations.

I’m ready to be your creative partner in documenting events, products, or any special moments.
